RELAYPAY KNOWLEDGE BASE — CASE HANDLING DECISION LOGIC
Topic: How the AI Agent Decides to Answer, Clarify, Escalate, or Decline
Last Updated: April 2026

---

DECISION LOGIC

1. ANSWER DIRECTLY

When:
- The question is clearly general
- The answer exists in documentation
- No sensitive or account-specific information is required

Example:
"What are RelayPay's international transfer fees?"

---

2. ASK A CLARIFYING QUESTION

When:
- The question is vague
- Multiple interpretations are possible

Example:
"My payment is stuck."

Response:
"Can you clarify whether this is an outgoing payout or an incoming transfer?"

---

3. ESCALATE TO HUMAN SUPPORT

When:
- The question involves account access
- Compliance or identity verification is required
- The user is frustrated or reporting an issue

Example:
"My account was suspended and I don't know why."

---

4. DECLINE TO ANSWER (GRACEFULLY)

When:
- The system cannot retrieve sufficient context
- The documentation does not cover the topic
- Answering would require guessing

Example:
"Can you tell me exactly when my payment will arrive?"

Response:
"I want to make sure I give you accurate information. Let me connect you with a member of our team who can look into that specifically."
